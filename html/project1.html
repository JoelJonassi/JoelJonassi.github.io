This project was developed as a university assignment for a software development course. The platform is designed to assist lawyers in organizing their cases in compliance with government regulations. Legal processes often have strict deadlines, and managing these manually can be challenging. This web platform was developed to automate and streamline the management of legal processes, ensuring timely compliance and efficient case management.


The project has two versions:
    Version 1: Developed using pure JavaScript.
    Version 2: Enhanced and redeveloped using Angular for better performance and scalability.
