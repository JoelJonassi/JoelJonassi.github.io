A complete tool created for the Adventist Church in São Mateus de Oliveira, Famalicão is the Register Members App. This application is intended to simplify the administration of Sabbath School participants and provides a range of functionalities to improve the effectiveness and involvement of classroom monitors. With this App Class monitors can:

  • Continually record Sabbath School participants' arrivals and departures.
  • Remind monitors of significant occasions, such birth, marriage, and baptism anniversaries, so they can offer well-wishes on time.
  • Get comprehensive historical reports in PDF format for every trimester with ease.
  • Use the app to conveniently make direct phone calls to members.
